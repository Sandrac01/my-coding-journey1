Why Coding!

I decided to take up on codig because learning to code is no longer a “speciality” skill. Rather, it has become a mainstream, core discipline utilized in nearly every industry; requiring more and more skilled workers every year.Coding tests a variety of abilities. It hones problem-solving and analysis skills, such as finding errors and thinking logically. Further, coding often helps people develop teamwork and interpersonal skills since software and application projects are often cross-disciplinary and collaborative. Coding is a skill that translates into many career paths and enhances many others; making professionals more versatile and valuable to employers.

  • Learning to code gives access to various career opportunities.Job prospects for coders extend far beyond technology companies. With coding knowledge and skills l am able to gain access to careers in healthcare, education, public sector (governmanet and social service) as well as private sector.
  • Projections for coding careers remain strong and web development is one of the on careers on demand. As the demand for web-developers keep growing, I am workig towards expandig my career in the field of technology.
  • Numerous programming languages to choose from and build a cereer. Whilst focusing on building websites l work with many programming languages and the main ones are HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Node js.
  • Coding also improves your critical thinking and creativity skills. As noted by Steve Jobs, coding teaches the mind to view problems from multiple perspectives and think creatively about solving them.
  • Another reason for choosing coding is how it enables you to find a more fulfilling career as it offers a path for professionals who are already in the workforce as well as those who are new to the job market.
